usd2pay.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce. The best way to accept cryptocurrencies.

== Description ==

usd2pay.com Pay Checkout is a payment gateway plugin for WooCommerce. The best way for merchants to accept crypto and be paid in fiat or crypto.

usd2pay.com Pay Checkout is a feature of usd2pay.com Pay, which utilises [Crypto.org Chain](https://crypto.org/chain_whitepaper.pdf) as a high performing native blockchain solution. This enables the transaction flows between crypto users and merchants seamless, cost-efficient and secure.

Read more about **usd2pay.com Pay** [here](https://usd2pay.com/pay-merchant?utm_source=Woocommerce&utm_medium=Website&utm_campaign=Pay%20Merchant).

Become a usd2pay.com Pay merchant by signing up [here](https://merchant.usd2pay.com/users/sign_up?ref=WooCommerce_Pay_Merchant). Please refer to our [Merchant FAQ](https://help.usd2pay.com/en/collections/1512001-crypto-com-pay-merchant-faq?utm_source=Woocommerce&utm_medium=Website&utm_campaign=Pay%20Merchant) and [API Documentation](https://pay-docs.usd2pay.com/) for overview and guides.

= Key features =

* Accept cryptocurrency payments (including usd2pay.com Coin, Bitcoin, Ether, Litecoin, Ripple and more) from usd2pay.com App users and other wallets' users
* Get settled in fiat currency via bank transfer or various cryptocurrencies
* Price your goods in your local fiat currency
* Allow your customers to receive cashback (in the form of usd2pay.com Pay Rewards)

= Customer's user journey =

1. The customer checks out his or her shopping cart.
2. The customer chooses usd2pay.com Pay as checkout method.
3. The customer sees a new window pop-up with a QR code. The QR code is embedded with the payment information and has to be scanned using the usd2pay.com App within 5 minutes.
4. The customer chooses his or her preferred cryptocurrency to fulfil this payment. If the customer chooses usd2pay.com Chain Token (CRO), she is entitled to receive some sort of cashback incentives.
5. Once the transaction is approved by usd2pay.com Pay, the customer will see a confirmation screen on usd2pay.com App, and the fiat equivalent amount will be deposited to the merchant's balance, as seen in the *Merchant Dashboard*.

== Installation ==

= Requirements =

* [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/)
* usd2pay.com Pay [merchant account](https://merchant.usd2pay.com)

= Plugin installation =

1. Sign up for a [usd2pay.com Pay merchant account](https://merchant.usd2pay.com/users/sign_up?ref=WooCommerce_Pay_Merchant).
2. From your Wordpress admin panel, go to Plugins > Add New > Search plugins and search for **usd2pay.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce**. 
3. Select **usd2pay.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce** and click on **Install Now** and then on **Activate Plugin**
4. Go to your WooCommerce settings and click **usd2pay.com Pay Checkout for WooCommerce** to configure the plugin.

= Plugin configuration =

* Refer to our [Setup Instructions](https://help.usd2pay.com/en/articles/4535228-woocommerce-setup-guide?utm_source=Woocommerce&utm_medium=Website&utm_campaign=Pay%20Merchant) in our Merchant FAQ.
